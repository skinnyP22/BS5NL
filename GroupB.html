England

  • Coach: Gareth Southgate
  • Captain: Harry Kane
  • Key Player: Harry Kane
  • Formation: 4-2-3-1

England are participating in their seventh consecutive World Cup but are looking for their first triumph since 1966. Could they end that agonising wait at Qatar 2022?

USA

  • Coach: Gregg Berhalter
  • Captain: Tyler Adams
  • Key Player: Cristian Pulisic
  • Formation: 4-3-3

The USMNT is at the World Cup for the 11th time in their history, back after they failed to qualify for the 2018 edition in Russia altogether. Berhalter and his men will be desperate to make amends for that performance by going far in Qatar and they sure have the talent to spring a surprise.

Iran

  • Coach: Carlos Queiroz
  • Captain: Ehsan Hajsafi
  • Key Player: Ehsan Hajsafi
  • Formation: 4-3-3

Iran will head into their third consecutive World Cup and will be aiming to make it out of the group stages for the first time in their history.

Wales

  • Coach: Robert Page
  • Captain: Gareth Bale
  • Key Player: Gareth Bale
  • Formation: 3-4-3

Wales will be making their second appearance at the World Cup in Qatar after a hiatus of 64 years. They made their first and only appearance in 1958, ending their journey at the quarter-final stage after losing to eventual winners Brazil.
