Brazil

  • Coach: Tite
  • Captain: Thiago Silva
  • Key Player: Neymar
  • Formation: 4-3-3

Five-time World Cup champions Brazil are the most successful team in the history of the competition, but 2002 was the last time the Selecao got their hands on the coveted trophy.

Switzerland

  • Coach: Murat Yakin
  • Captain: Granit Xhaka
  • Key Player: Xherdan Shaqiri
  • Formation: 4-2-3-1

Switzerland are playing in their fifth consecutive World Cup, having survived the group stages in three of their last four attempts. They shocked reigning European champions Italy in their qualification group to book their ticket for the tournament.

Cameroon

  • Coach: Rigobert Song
  • Captain: Vincent Aboubakar
  • Key Player: Karl Toko Ekambi
  • Formation: 4-3-3

Cameroon are set to take part in their eighth World Cup later this year after missing out on the previous edition in Russia. They booked their ticket for Qatar after seeing off Algeria in the qualification play-offs.

Serbia

  • Coach: Dragan Stojković
  • Captain: Dušan Tadić
  • Key Player: Sergej Milinković-Savić
  • Formation: 3-4-1-2

Serbia will be determined to show the world what they can do at the upcoming World Cup after missing out on Euro 2020. They saw off the likes of Portugal among others in qualification to prove their credentials, but a difficult task awaits in Qatar come November.
