Portugal

  • Coach: Fernando Santos
  • Captain: Cristiano Ronaldo
  • Key Player: Bruno Fernandes
  • Formation: 4-2-2-2

Portugal are making their eighth appearance at the World Cup in Qatar and coach Fernando Santos will hope that his team can live up to the expectations. They are yet to lift the coveted prize and their best result was a third-place finish in 1966.

Uruguay

  • Coach: Diego Alonso
  • Captain: Diego Godin
  • Key Player: Luis Suarez
  • Formation: 4-4-2

Uruguay qualified for the upcoming 2022 World Cup after finishing third in the CONMEBOL qualifiers behind Brazil and Argentina. They have a rich history at the tournament, hosting the first World Cup in 1930 and going on to win it after emerging triumphant over Argentina in the final.

South Korea

  • Coach: Paulo Bento
  • Captain: Son Heung-min
  • Key Player: Son Heung-min
  • Formation: 4-4-2

South Korea made their World Cup debut in 1954 and have qualified for every edition since 1986, with their best-ever finish coming in 2002 when they reached the semi-finals

Ghana

  • Coach: Otto Addo
  • Captain: André Ayew
  • Key Player: Iñaki Williams
  • Formation: 4-1-4-1

Ghana will want to make the most of the 2022 World Cup after missing out on the 2018 edition. New boss Otto Addo took charge during the country's final push for qualification and got the team over the line as they held Nigeria to a 1-1 draw in the final round to clinch their ticket for Qatar based on away goals.
